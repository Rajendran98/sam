Who we are?

SecqurAIse Technologies is an early stage Artificial Intelligence innovator. Our mission is to bring complex technologies within the reach of every business by creating simple solutions for day-to-day issues. Our AI powered & self-learning technology enables us to offer higher value at ultra-low cost.

What’s our culture?

Just like our technology, our culture and work ethics are evolving continuously. Our desire is to create an adaptive work place that enables everybody to excel at what they are doing by eliminating the irritants. We don’t have forums, committees, and long meandering meetings. At SecqurAIse, everything revolves around one key fulcrum, Purpose. We practice what we preach. Our culture is self-tutoring too and uses behavioural science, feedback loops and community feeds to keep evolving with time.

What are we looking for?

We are looking for candidates who have the eye for details and who are willing to take the ball and run with it. We love having people with fantastic Analytical and Logic skills around us.

We prefer T-shaped expertise to I-shaped ones — people with deep expertise in one area and broad interests in other areas rather than people with high skill in a narrow field.

The most important characteristic we seek in our future colleagues is resourcefulness to figure out what should be done and get it done – an ability to combine result oriented thinking with nifty action. This is absolutely essential for the small start-up team that we are and hence we would prefer your candidature if you have already worked in a start-up and have demonstrated such characteristics before.


How do we build products?

We are a small team, and everyone plays a vital part in evolving the product. We expect you to feel for the product, be sensitive to the need to maintain a balance between performance, quality and time to market as the authority to decide what to build, how to build and when to build, to a larger extent will be with you.

Everyone in the organization plays an active role at each stage of the product development from design and feature prioritization to release and validation. We practice continuous development and follow the Agile Scrum model to track and manage product development in an extremely efficient way. Life at SecqurAIse revolves around Scrums. We proactively resolve bottlenecks by visually tracking progress using Kanban boards.

How can you get in touch with us?

If you think you can relate to our belief at SecqurAIse and have the skills we are looking for, and would like to spend your productive time on helping us build this awesome product, please write to us at jobs@secquraise.com.

We are a bootstrapped start-up and we offer competitive pay, equity and comprehensive benefits. However, we do not believe negotiation skills should be a factor in determining your salary.
