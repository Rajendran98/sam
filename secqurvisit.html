SECQURVISIT

This user friendly system can aid the organization in safeguarding against transgressions by visitors while offering a superlative experience as they enter and exit facilities. This Visitor Management System (VMS) gives a unified view of inflow and outflow to any facility.


This Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered solution offers a host of features to enable faster admission process, reducing the time spent by visitors at the entry. Thanks to the efficient and user friendly process, the security guard at the gate can handle visitor authorization eliminating the need for dedicated manpower at reception.

The system works with multiple access control mechanisms including Face Recognition, Biometric Reader, RFID and QR code to scrutinize visitors as they attempt to enter the premises eliminating manual errors and impersonation.

Type Supported

  • Visitors with prior invitation from hosts.

  • Cold calling visitors without prior invitations.

  • Delivery/Dispatch vehicles entering for handling material.

  • Contract/Temporary employees who visit for short/long durations.

  • Employees from other branches visiting for short durations.

  • Contractors visiting for job works.
